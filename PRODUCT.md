# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, game studio interviewers, collaborators, and friends who open the site on desktop to quickly understand Wang Juntao's game design, programming, AI-native prototyping, and portfolio direction.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio is a playable first impression. It should show personality and technical taste before the visitor reads details, then guide them into experience, selected projects, strengths, and contact information.

## Brand Personality

Pixel, technical, restrained, playful.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a generic template portfolio, a heavy marketing landing page, or a blurry glass UI. Avoid random black masks, muddy particles, decorative clutter, and effects that break the authored interactive feel.

## Design Principles

Make the first screen feel interactive, not merely decorative.

Keep special effects modular so visual experiments can be switched without destroying the current working version.

Prefer crisp pixel edges, deliberate motion, and readable hierarchy over soft blur and noisy ornament.

Let the site feel like a game developer's interface: direct, tactile, and a little surprising.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keep text readable on dark backgrounds, preserve keyboard access for navigation and the editor, and provide reduced-motion fallbacks for animated effects where practical.
